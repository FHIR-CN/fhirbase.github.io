Vision of FHIRbase

FHIRbase provides you a storage to develop solutions for Health IT developers. For systems like EHRs, Patient Portals, etc.
FHIRbase designed in accordance with the FHIR standard.

The Heart of FHIRbase is PostgreSQL

FHIRbase is specifically designed extension of PostgreSQL - "the worldâ€™s most advanced open source database" - offering you all transactional integrity, ACID, richness of SQL query language and document structure out of the box.

The Soul of FHIRbase is FHIR standard

FHIR standard gives specific direction to exchange structured medical data and FHIRbase is developed to easily store and retrieve medical data in FHIR format.

The Face of FHIRbase is a simple API

A simple and straightforward API gives developers ability to use accustomed languages and platforms, such as Java, Node.js, .NET, Ruby, etc., to interact with FHIRbase.
